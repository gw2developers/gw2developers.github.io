Shadowblade.6350:

18 Sep 2015

Hello,

As suggested by the Community Manager I contacted by email, I post my problem in this forum to get some help.

Two years ago, I created and published an Android app based on the World versus World API, which displayed the battlefield current state to the user : GuildWars 2 WvW Battle Map. Later, I renamed it WvW Assistant.

One week ago, I published an update to this app on Google Play to provide a better user interface and a better user experience, but their team decided to suspend the app from the store because of the following reason :

REASON FOR SUSPENSION:Violation of the impersonation or deceptive behavior (https://play.google.com/about/developer-content-policy.html#impersonation) provisions of the Content Policy. Please refer to the impersonation policy help article (https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/2986097) for more information.

If I understand it correctly, the Google Play team suspects me to use the GuildWars 2 brand without the company permission.

That’s not the first time they suspend one of my GuildWars 2 related apps from the store, always for the same reason.
I tried to ask them what specific details they wanted to see modified, but they never gave the response.

As a consequence, all my GW2 related apps are suspended from Google Play, and my Google Play Developer account has also been suspended.

My only hope to resolve this problem is to send a document to the Google Play team, which proves that ArenaNet is OK with me publishing GW2 related apps on Google Play.
To be clear, this is the description of the kind of document they request :
Please provide a document that proves you have the rights to use specific content, icons, images, etc.

Has anyone here had the same problem ?

Can anyone from the staff provide me with an official document that I could send to the Google Play team ?
