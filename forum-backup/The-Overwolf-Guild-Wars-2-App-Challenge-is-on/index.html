Hey there!

Name’s Raif, I’m the Overwolf Community Manager. Overwolf already has world boss timers and WvW camp timers and such created by developers from past contests which you can see on the Appstore.

As much as we’d love to have a Mac client it’s not something we have planned for the near future. A lot of the tech needed to do what Overwolf does would have to be re-written and it would take a very very long time to do that even if we were to start today. Having a Mac client is something we want to do eventually, but it’s not in the cards at this time.
