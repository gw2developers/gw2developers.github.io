Raif.9507:

17 Feb 2014

Hey all!

My name is Raif, I’m the Community Manager over at Overwolf.

Just some info on Overwolf: We’re a software platform that overlays into your game (just graphically, doesn’t violate GW2 TOS) and gives you some cool apps in game like a video recorder, Teamspeak overlay, music player, Facebook chat, direct uploads to YouTube or Facebook, a Twitch streaming app and some GW2 apps like WvW timers and guides. You can check us out at www.Overwolf.com

We’re looking for some new ideas as far as apps for GW2 for people to develop (even you guys, we have an open SDK so anyone can make an app using the GW2 API and use our SDK to make an app for the platform) and there is no one better to ask then you all here. We want to get ideas for things that you guys would use and like to see so that people who want to develop apps can make them.

What kind of apps do you wish you had in game for GW2? Let us know!

My apologies for the late reply, but you can bring the whole whole TS into your game without tabbing out. On the TS app you have the option for “Full TS3” which brings the whole TS3 window into your game or the “Channel” button which gives you just your channel.

As far as I remember, the TS overlay made by the TS guys only showed the current channel not the whole TS (remember please we are 2 different companies).

As for seeing how many people are in your channel do you mean a number? Or a list of people as that can be shown by the “Channel” button. If you are looking for a number then that is something different.

The font color is something I can pass on to the devs and see if we can implement something like that, but the main reason it is white is due to the black background of the overlay itself as a lot of other colors are very difficult to see on that color background.
